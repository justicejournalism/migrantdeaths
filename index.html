Arizona Migrahack, March 20-22, 2015

Migrant deaths along the AZ border, 2001-2013

Team: Humane Borders

Developer: Mike Kreyche mkreyche@gmail.com

Object: Display year to year patterns of migratory movement across the desert based on locations of migrant deaths.

Data from the Arizona OpenGIS Initiative for Deceased Migrants, a joint project of Humane Borders and the Pima County Office of the Medical Examiner
